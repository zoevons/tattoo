Our Studio

We offer a range of different services here at White Rabbit Tattoos.
Please have a look below and feel free to get in touch with us anytime.

Tattoos

We have an artist for every tattoo desire! All small tattoos we price up individually and all bigger works are charged by hourly or a day session rate.

Book an appointment

Piercings

We offer a range of body piercings. Prices are dependent on body placement. We don't currently offer walk ins so please book first. please book first


Book an appointment

Laser Removal

If you are interested in getting your tattoo removed or faded to get covered with something new then we can help you. Interested to know more?

Learn more here

Consultations

Consultations are available between 10:30am - 11am. You don't need to book however if you want a particular artist we advise you to contact us before to make sure that the artist is available that day.

Appointments

To book an appointment you will need to secure it with a deposit. We can only give you a very rough estimate online. Waiting times are different with each artist and time of the year.
